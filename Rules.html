Rules

  • Respect: All deputies must respect citizens and every department. No department is higher than the other. You are all equal and will be punished equally if you attempt to start a department war.
  • Abuse of weapons: Abuse of any kind is intolerable and will result in punishment from the assigned department.
  • Abuse of radio: Using the radio incorrectly will result in a possible suspension depending on the severity from your assigned department.
  • Uniform: All departments are to have uniform on. Packages, unrealistic skin colors, and t-shirts that are not provided by Mano County should be removed.
  • Maturity: All departments should be mature while on duty. No faces are allowed.
  • Driving: All departments must follow all road laws, and drive safely. No exceptions on rank. All departments also must pull over for other responding emergency vehicles, NOT FOR DOT, unless you're responding.
  • Detainment: MCSO and PSP are REQUIRED to state reason of arrest/detain.
