Charges

Infraction- A violation of a traffic law(s). May result in a warning or a citation.

Misdemeanor- An offense that is not classified as a Felony. May result in a fine or possible arrest depending on the severity.

Felony- A serious charge, typically involves violence or terror. Greater than a misdemeanor, and definitely punishable by time in prison.
